GAMES

LEGBREAKER

Genre: Puzzle Platformer | Engine: Godot | Development Period: July 2019 - June 2020 | Initial Release: January 2020

In Legbreaker, you control an unwitting test subject in a suspicious laboratory. An experiment has granted the player character an extraordinary vertical leap that breaks one of their legs each time, meaning each level must be cleared in just two jumps.

The game was my first attempt at a full-featured release after some prototyping and experimentation with other ideas in the Godot engine. After working on the game for several months, I submitted it to the Finally Finish Something 2020 jam on itch.io, where it earned third place. That, in addition to YouTube coverage by popular accounts earned the game a brief feature in Kotaku and 23.4k downloads on itch.io to date.

THE STORY OF DRADEN

Genres: Simulation, Action, Role-Playing | Engine: Unity | Development Period: March 2024 - April 2024 | Initial Release: April 2024

In The Story of Draden, you play as a medieval villager who has been transformed into a dragon by a magical spell. The player is tasked with improving the town during the day and fending off waves of bandits at night for the three days it will take to undo the curse.

This game was developed over six weeks as the final project of a Unity game development course. Working in a group of four, I was able to specialize on the visuals of the game. I created the game's hand-drawn assets and programmed animations, a part-swapping sprite system for NPCs, and the game's main head's up display. All of these contributions besides the user interface elements are showcased well in the game's trailer!
